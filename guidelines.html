■COMMISSION RULES

Please read these carefully.
By commissioning me you accept and bound to these terms.
■Commission Rules
1. Prepayment 100% after sketch is accepted.
2. First come, first served.
3. In general it takes a minimum of 7 days or 2 weeks to finish.
4. References - I need reference images for the character. Or specific explanation of the details. Appreciated references: appearance with 3 images or more, pose, background (if exist), short explanation of specific things (less than 300 words), canvas size.
5. It would be appreciated if you can provide purpose, deadline or budget.
6. Revisions - I accept corrections up to twice for each process (sketch & coloring).
7. I have my own style and cannot imitate other artist's style.
8. I may use the finished artwork in any way. ( promoting myself, reposting, etc. ) unless asked otherwise.
9. I may stream or post the progress of the commission unless asked otherwise.
10. You must not use your commission for any commercial purposes, to produce merchandise, or in any means to make profit if it is not ordered with commercial purpose commission

■PAYMENTS

Prepayment 100%.

1. Payment made through PayPal.
2. I accept client with legal age [18+] and use their own money, credit and debit card. If you are not legal, at least you use your own money, credit and debit card.
3. If the commission is cancelled due to issues on the commissioner’s end, then the commissioner will not receive refunds once payments are received.
4. If the commission is cancelled due to issues on the artist’s end, then the commissioner is entitled to a full refund.
5. Once the artwork has been paid in full and delivered via online, all sales are final.
6. Pay with Goods(item) or Service payment.
